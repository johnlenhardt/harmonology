Caroling, Caroling
Harmonaires Christmas Show

With the support of high-desert Community Chorus, The Hi-Country Harmonaires performed Caroling, Caroling in December, along with numerous shorter Christmas singouts around the Victor Valley including the Victorville Tree Lighting Ceremony. the Elves Workhop and the Aspen Lodge Tree Lighting.


Below are videos of the Caroling, Caroling show at the Mariposa Lodge December 22nd.


(Scroll down...)

Caroling, Caroling
The Community Chorus

The Harmonaires put out a call for community members to help us with our Christmas show. Ten singers stepped forward to join the fun. The group consisted of some trained singers and some that claimed they never sang in a group, before! In addition, the Harmonaires enjoyed the addition of female voices to the sound. The sound was awesome! Enjoy!


Be sure to watch full-screen!

Caroling, Caroling
Tuesday Afternoon Quartet

The Harmonaires have a number of new members. Two of our newest members teamed up with two of our "old-timers" to form Tuesday Afternoon. (So named because that was their rehearsal day...)

With only six weeks of rehearsal our newest quartet treated us to their renditions of two Christmas favorites.

Caroling, Caroling
The Hi-Country Harmonaires #1

The Hi-Country Harmonaires Barbershop Chorus performed in two sections of Caroling, Caroling. This section of the show was secular Christmas favorites. 

Caroling, Caroling
Harmonology Barbershop Quartet

Harmonology lost their Bass of almost three years in March of 2018. Instead of disbanding, they found a new Lead in a new chorus member and two of the quartet moved to different positions. In essense they are a brand new quartet. Harmonology had fun sharing four Christmas numbers.

Caroling, Caroling
Blended not Stirred Quartet

We all had a real treat for the last show. Our director, Mike Smith, is a member of Blended not Stirred. Because of work scheduling difficulties, the opportunity to hear their velvet tones is rare and appreciated! They treated us to a selection you will definately enjoy!

Caroling, Caroling
Hi-Country Harmonaires Finale

The Harmonaires closed the show with a few more religious songs with the assistance of Blended not Stirred.
