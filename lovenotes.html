The Hi-Country Harmonaires opened the show with Hello Mary Lou, Sweet Adeline,  Heart of my Heart, Honey/Little Lize,  and You Tell Me Your Dream.

The new chapter quartet (in search for a name) sang Sweet and Lovely, I'll Forget You and Lida Rose/Will I Ever Tell You assisted by Kirstie Acosta.

The Hi-Country Harmonaires closed the show singing When She Loved Me, Let Me Call You Sweetheart, Sweet, Sweet Roses of Morn, Down by the Old Mill Stream and Wait 'Til the Sun Shines Nellie.
